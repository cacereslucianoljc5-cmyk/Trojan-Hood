How it works

From zero to your first fill in four steps.

  1. 01

    Open the bot

    Send /start to @Trojan_Hood_bot and create a new wallet or import yours with its private key.

  2. 02

    Fund your wallet

    Send WETH to buy and a bit of ETH for gas to your address.

  3. 03

    Paste the CA

    Paste the token's contract address and tap a buy button: 0.01 / 0.05 / 0.1 / 0.5 WETH or a custom amount.

  4. 04

    Track your PnL

    See your position with its profit/loss % in the menu. Sell 25 / 50 / 100% or withdraw whenever you want.

Everything you need to trade

Six parts, one terminal — built to feel like a product, not a script.

Self-custodial wallet

You control the keys. They're stored encrypted with AES-256-GCM; the seed phrase is shown once and never saved.

Smart V2 + V3 routing

Quotes Uniswap V2 and V3 in parallel (fee tiers 0.01–1%) and executes via the best route. Buys tokens that V2-only bots can't.

Paste the CA and buy

Instant panel with name, symbol, reference price, route and balances. Preset or custom amount buttons.

Positions with PnL

A list of your tokens with their profit/loss %. Green wins, Red loses.

Pool → token automatically

Paste a pool address (what you copy from DEX Screener) and the bot resolves it to the underlying token.

Withdrawals and settings

Withdraw ETH or WETH to any external address. Adjust slippage and the button amounts to your liking.

Built on Robinhood Chain

Robinhood's public Layer 2 (Arbitrum-style, on Ethereum). Gas is paid in ETH and every pool quotes against WETH — that's why the bot's base currency is WETH, the network's native currency in its ERC-20 form.
